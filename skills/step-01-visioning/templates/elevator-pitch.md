### 🤖 AGENT DIRECTIVE (HIDDEN FROM FINAL OUTPUT)
When the user requests an Elevator Pitch for an idea, strictly follow these processing steps before generating the final output:
1. **Analyze the Idea:** Identify the target customer, their core problem, and the proposed solution.
2. **Apply "2 Brains (Tell It and Sell It)" technique:** 
   - *Tell It (Practical):* What exactly does the product do? (e.g., automated RNG ticketing).
   - *Sell It (Emotional):* How does it make the user feel? (e.g., peace of mind, thrill of winning safely).
3. **Format using the Geoffrey Moore Template:** Fill in the strict template below.
4. **Distill:** Condense the template output into a 1-2 sentence compelling Product Vision Statement.
5. **Output Generation:** Only output the "ELEVATOR PITCH (GEOFFREY MOORE FORMAT)" and "DISTILLED PRODUCT VISION STATEMENT" sections to the user.

---

### 📋 ELEVATOR PITCH (GEOFFREY MOORE FORMAT)
*   **FOR** [Target customer segment]
*   **WHO** [Statement of the need or opportunity / Nỗi đau hoặc cơ hội]
*   **THE** [Product name] **is a** [Product category]
*   **THAT** [Key benefit, compelling reason to buy / Lợi ích cốt lõi]
*   **UNLIKE** [Primary competitive alternative / Đối thủ cạnh tranh chính]
*   **OUR PRODUCT** [Statement of primary differentiation / Điểm khác biệt độc nhất]

### 🎯 DISTILLED PRODUCT VISION STATEMENT
*[Agent: Combine the practical and emotional elements into a 1-2 sentence, high-impact vision statement suitable for the Product Owner to communicate to the Scrum Team and Stakeholders.]*
