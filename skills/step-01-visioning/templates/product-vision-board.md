### 🤖 AGENT DIRECTIVE (HIDDEN FROM FINAL OUTPUT)
When the user requests a Product Vision Board, execute the following reasoning steps:
1. **Analyze the Input:** Extract the core idea, target users, their problems, proposed solutions, and business benefits from the user's prompt.
2. **Map to the 5 Pillars (Roman Pichler's Format):**
   - *Vision:* The ultimate purpose or overarching goal (the North Star).
   - *Target Group:* The specific market segment, consumers, or buyers.
   - *Needs:* The problems solved or benefits provided to the Target Group.
   - *Product:* The 3-5 key features that make the product stand out.
   - *Value (Business Goals):* How this product benefits the producer/sponsoring organization (e.g., revenue, cost reduction, compliance).
3. **Output Generation:** Output ONLY the "PRODUCT VISION BOARD" section below in a clean, professional Markdown format.
