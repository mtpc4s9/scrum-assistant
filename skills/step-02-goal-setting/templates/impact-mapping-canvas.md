# Impact Mapping Canvas

This template helps the Scrum Team map the relationship between the Product Goal, the target actors, the behavioral changes required, and the high-level deliverables.

---

## 🎯 1. Product Goal (WHY?)
*Define the single, measurable Product Goal (commitment for the Product Backlog).*
> **Goal Statement:** "Achieve [quantifiable target] in [specific metric] by [target date]."
*   **Primary Metric:** [e.g., Active users, transaction success rate, response time]
*   **Baseline:** [Current value]
*   **Target:** [Desired value]

---

## 👥 2. Actors (WHO?)
*Who can produce the effect? Who can obstruct it? Who are the users? Who is impacted?*
*   **Primary Actors:** [The main users or beneficiaries of the goal]
*   **Secondary Actors:** [Supporting roles, e.g., Operations, Support, Partners]
*   **Off-Stage / External Actors:** [e.g., Compliance Auditors, Payment Providers, Regulatory Bodies]

---

## ⚡ 3. Impacts (HOW?)
*How should the actors' behavior change? How can they help us achieve the goal? How can they obstruct us?*
*   **Actor A:**
    *   *Desired Behavior:* [e.g., Complete registration in under 2 minutes]
    *   *Obstacle to mitigate:* [e.g., Abandoning signup due to long forms]
*   **Actor B:**
    *   *Desired Behavior:* [e.g., Flag suspicious transactions immediately]

---

## 📦 4. Deliverables (WHAT?)
*What can we do as a organization or team to support the required behavioral changes? (These become your high-level Backlog Features/Epics).*
*   **To support Actor A's behavior change:**
    *   [ ] Feature 1: [e.g., Social login integration]
    *   [ ] Feature 2: [e.g., Auto-fill address fields]
*   **To support Actor B's behavior change:**
    *   [ ] Feature 3: [e.g., Real-time compliance alert dashboard]

---

## 🛡️ Guardrails & Alignment Check
*   **No Orphaned Deliverables:** Every Feature/Epic listed in Section 4 must link directly back to an Impact in Section 3 and an Actor in Section 2.
*   **Socratic Review:** Ask the team: *"If we build all these deliverables, are we certain they will change the behaviors of the actors in the way we expect to achieve the goal?"*
